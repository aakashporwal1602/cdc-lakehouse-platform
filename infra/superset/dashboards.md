# Superset dashboards

Build these four dashboards on the imported Gold datasets:

1. **Customer Analytics** — `customer_360`: LTV distribution, value-segment mix,
   tier migration, cohort tenure, top customers.
2. **Sales Dashboard** — `sales_daily` + `fct_sales`: daily revenue trend,
   buyers, average ticket, payment-method split.
3. **Revenue** — `revenue_daily` + `revenue_by_segment`: net vs. captured vs.
   refunded, revenue by segment/tier.
4. **Inventory & Orders** — `inventory_health` + `order_funnel` +
   `fulfillment_sla`: stockout risk heatmap, reorder list, order funnel,
   delivery-rate SLA.
